## Hints:
**Ancient Formulas**

Numbers dance in patterns old as computing itself. Seeds grow into streams, predictable yet chaotic. Three constants guide their path - multiplier, increment, ceiling. What algorithm from the dawn of randomness follows this trinity?

**Divided Guardians**

Two fragments, two masks of different natures. One wears the answer to life, the universe, and everything. The other, a checkerboard pattern in binary. Together they shield the master from prying eyes.

**The Vessel's Secrets**

The vessel begins with the initials of its creator. Then follows a shadow of the starting point, twisted by the guardian's first half. Size matters - how many bytes to read? Only then does the true cipher text begin its whispers.

**Layered Illusions**

Not one but two walls protect the message. The inner wall flows like water, changing with each step. The outer wall stands firm, divided into equal blocks. A standard protector, its name found in many encryption suites.

*The inner wall prizes perfect squares, then shifts them beyond the ordinary.*
